ONE-PUNCH-MAN

ABOUT

One-Punch Man tells the story of Saitama, a superhero who can defeat any opponent with a single punch, life gets pretty boring when you can beat the snot out of any villain with just one punch. This average-looking guy has a not-so-average problem—he just can’t seem to find an opponent strong enough to take on!

Saitama. The title character, Saitama (サイタマ), is a bald-headed 26-year-old man who is bored of fighting because he is effortlessly able to defeat enemies with a single punch. He lives in an apartment in City Z
